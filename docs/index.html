Delivered

Delivered is PHP package for Laravel applications sending mission critical communications. Businesses can monitor and track their email deliverability performance across applications, in real-time, ensuring that critical communication reaches its intended recipients.

Principles

The key principles behind Delivered are:

  • Simplicity: Easy-to-use interface with minimal setup required.
  • Transparency: Full visibility of email deliverability and related metrics.
  • Flexibility: Compatible with Laravel applications and integrates smoothly with Mailgun.
  • Efficiency: Offers real-time reporting on email statuses, including open and click tracking.

Functionality

Delivered provides:

  • Real-time email status updates through webhooks.
  • Comprehensive reports on email deliverability with tracking for sending, delivery, opens, clicks, and bounces.
  • Easy integration with Laravel through a simple package installation.
  • Detailed logs for email events, offering insights into user engagement and email performance.

Why Delivered is Useful

Email deliverability is a critical aspect of maintaining communication with customers and users. With Delivered, you can be confident that your emails are reaching their destination. By tracking key metrics like open rates, click rates, and bounce rates, you can optimize your email campaigns for better performance.

Integration and Usage

Delivered integrates directly into your Laravel application, and through its simple commands and webhook-driven system, it ensures that you're always aware of the status of your email communications. This helps reduce downtime and improve the reliability of your email systems.
